Club Championship

A unique feature of the EWC was the Club Championship, a cross-game competition where clubs earned points based on their performance in individual tournaments. The top 16 clubs shared the $20 million prize pool, with the first-place club receiving $7 million.

MASSIVE PRICE POOL

The event boasted a record-breaking prize pool of $62.5 million, with $34.3 million allocated to tournament winners and $20 million distributed among the top 16 clubs based on their overall performance.
